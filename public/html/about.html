About the Author

Carolyn Hannan Bell is a Licensed Professional Counselor in Cherry Hill, New Jersey. She specializes in counseling those that suffer the adverse affects of substance abuse. Anxiety, depression, and codependency are all common reactions to dysfunctional relationships. In her practice, Carolyn uses cognitive behavioral techniques, years of experience in the field, and the occasional animal companion to help individuals overcome these and other crippling issues.

As an author, Carolyn's aim is to provide much-needed narratives for children and adults suffering in dysfunctional relationships. Each narrative is designed to give a more intuitive and simplistic understanding of some central issue. Carolyn hopes to empower children and families everywhere by shining a light into the darkness of emotional conflict.

Carolyn earned her Bachelor's Degree in Psychology from Duquesne University and her Master's Degree in Counseling from Chestnut Hill College.
